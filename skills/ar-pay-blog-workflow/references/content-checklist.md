# Content Checklist

Use before finalizing a plan or draft.

## Check
- Is the article useful in the first 2 paragraphs?
- Is the target reader obvious?
- Is the offer or takeaway clear?
- Is the CTA specific?
- Are headings scannable?
- Are there unsupported claims?
- Is the tone natural instead of keyword-stuffed?
- Is there a clear reason this belongs on AR-PAY blog?
