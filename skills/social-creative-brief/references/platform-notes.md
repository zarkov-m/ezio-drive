# Platform Notes

Use lightweight reminders, not hard laws.

## Instagram
- Strong first-frame hook matters.
- Keep text readable on mobile.
- Carousels need a slide-by-slide flow.

## Facebook
- Clarity beats visual complexity.
- Paid placements may need safer text density.

## Stories / Reels covers
- Keep key subject away from edges.
- Make CTA readable fast.

## Banners / website promos
- Message hierarchy matters more than decoration.
- Leave breathing room around CTA and logo.
